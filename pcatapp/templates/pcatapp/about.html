About

PharmSchoolSearch is a user-friendly, self-researched database which assists prospective pharmacy school students to find their perfect pharmacy school. It allows them to search schools using various criteria intending to provide applicants the information to evaluate their chances in the upcoming admissions cycle. We hope to give applicants meaningful information in a simple presentation so they can get to focusing on the important things like applications and essays. We aim to present the latest information by continuously compiling and updating our database, and reviewing all feedback to provide the best user-experience possible.
